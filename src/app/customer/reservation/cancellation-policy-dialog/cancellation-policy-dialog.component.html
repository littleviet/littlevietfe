CANCELLATION POLICY

In reservations of 8 people or more we will request your credit card data, as guarantee.
The procedure of reservation does not imply any charge on your credit card.
You will be able to modify or annul your reservation without any charge until 24 hours before it.
If you annul the reservation the same day or you do not come, 25€ per person will be charged.
You will be able to adjust the definitive number of diners until 6 hours before the reservation, on the contrary, 25€ will be charged for every person that does not assist.
For your tranquillity we always confirm (via email or sms) double check of your reservation: one of the moment of doing it and another the previous day.
